### AUTUMN KNIGHT
Autumn Knight is a pet project created by me for my love for D&D and the RPG game genre!

Game was created using Phaser.js, my first ever big step in game development! 

Free game assets taken from opengameart.org and craftpix.net! Check them out! 

https://craftpix.net/freebies/free-knight-character-sprites-pixel-art/?num=1&count=100&sq=knight&pos=4
https://opengameart.org/

As Knight: Press directional buttons to move around, A to attack, Space to block, and E to switch classes!
As Mage: Press directional buttons to move around, A to attack, Space to block,Q to cast magic and E to switch classes!
